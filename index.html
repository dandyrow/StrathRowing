Who we are

Strathclyde University Boat Club has over 50 years of history rowing on the River Clyde. We have grown from humble origins and continue to be a completely student-run club.

We compete at every category from novice level through to championship. We welcome rowers of all abilities and experience levels, as well as those who have yet to begin their rowing careers.
